About NAV JYOTI PUBLIC SCHOOL KISHORPUR

NAV JYOTI PUBLIC SCHOOL KISHORPUR was established in 1997 and it is managed by the Pvt. Unaided. It is located in Rural area. It is located in PALWAL block of PALWAL district of HARYANA. The school consists of Grades from 1 to 12. The school is Co-educational and it have an attached pre-primary section. The school is Not Applicable in nature and is not using school building as a shift-school. Hindi is the medium of instructions in this school. This school is approachable by all weather road. In this school academic session starts in April. The school has Private building. All the classrooms are in good condition. The school has Pucca boundary wall. The school has have electric connection.The school has a playground. The school has a library and has 365 books in its library. The school has 4 computers for teaching and learning purposes and all are functional. The school is having a computer aided learning lab.
